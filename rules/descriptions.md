## short_description

- State who they were and why they are known.
- Written for quick tooltip reading.

## long_description

- 2-4 short paragraphs.
- Explain who they were, what they did, and their historical context.
- Focus on the most important achievements and relationships.
- Written for a general audience.
- Casual, relaxed style.

## why_they_matter

- 1-2 short paragraphs.
- Explain their lasting impact on history, culture, science, politics, or society.
- Focus on why people still remember them today.
- Explain the person's lasting influence. Focus on what changed because of their work, ideas, discoveries, actions, or example, rather than simply listing what they created or accomplished.

## personality

- 1-3 sentences.
- Describe widely accepted personality traits, working style, or reputation.
- Avoid speculation and psychological diagnosis.
- Use historical accounts when possible.
