      About

      An interactive graph of historical figures. People are positioned by birth year (x-axis) and geographic region (y-axis). Node size reflects historical prominence according to HPI score by Pantheon dataset.

      Data source

      People, birth and death years, and node sizes come from the Pantheon dataset. Small modifications have been made when using the data.

      Yu, A. Z., et al. (2016). Pantheon 1.0, a manually verified dataset of globally famous biographies. Scientific Data 2:150075. doi: 10.1038/sdata.2015.75

      Pantheon by Datawheel is licensed under a Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike 4.0 International License.

      AI-generated content

      Display names, descriptions, connections between people and timelines are mostly AI-generated (Claude Code by Anthropic).

      There are likely historical errors and many historical claims are debatable.

      Data is incomplete, there is a lot of missing descriptions and relations.
